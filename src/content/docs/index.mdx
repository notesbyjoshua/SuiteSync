## Roommates by chance. Suitemates by design.

Your residential community is a big part of the YYGS experience. SuiteSync learns what helps you feel comfortable, then uses those preferences to create balanced groups of 4–6 students.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Tell us about you" icon="pencil">
    Complete a short survey about your routines, shared-space preferences, and what you hope for in a suite community.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Thoughtful matching" icon="puzzle">
    After the survey closes, responses will help form balanced suites—not rank applicants or decide YYGS admission.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Meet your suite" icon="comment">
    Once groups are released, you’ll be able to connect with your suitemates before arriving on campus.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
